Our Cookie Policy

Understanding how we use cookies to enhance your spicy journey.

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how CingeBites ("we", "us", or "our") uses them on our website, and how you can manage your cookie preferences. By continuing to use our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information and assist with personalization. Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your personal computer or mobile device when you go offline, while session cookies are deleted as soon as you close your web browser.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several reasons, including:

  • Essential Website Functionality: Some cookies are necessary for our website to function properly. This includes enabling navigation, securing areas of the website, and remembering items in your shopping cart (if applicable).
  • Performance and Analytics: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This data helps us improve the performance and usability of our site. For example, we might use cookies to track which pages are most popular or if users encounter error messages.
  • Personalization: We use cookies to remember your preferences and settings, such as your language choice or region, to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Advertising: Cookies may be used by us or our third-party advertising partners to deliver advertisements relevant to your interests, both on our website and across the internet. They also help us measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.
  • Social Media Features: Our website may include social media features, such as "Like" or "Share" buttons. These features may collect your IP address and which page you are visiting on our site, and may set a cookie to enable the feature to function properly.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • First-party cookies: These are cookies set by the website you are visiting (CingeBites.com). They are generally used for essential website functions or to remember your preferences.
  • Third-party cookies: These cookies are set by a domain other than the one you are visiting. This often happens when a website incorporates elements from other sites, such as images, social media plugins, or advertising. Third-party cookies are primarily used for cross-site tracking, ad-serving, and analytics.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie preferences by clicking on the appropriate opt-in/opt-out links provided in the cookie consent banner, or by setting your preferences within your web browser. Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences.

Browser Settings:

You can typically find these settings in the "options" or "preferences" menu of your browser. Refer to your browser's help menu for more detailed instructions on how to manage your cookie settings:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note that if you choose to block or delete cookies, some parts of our website may not function correctly or may not be accessible.
