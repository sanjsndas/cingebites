Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit a recipe to CingeBites?
We love recipe submissions! Please email your recipe to submissions@cingebites.com with "Recipe Submission" in the subject line. Include ingredients, instructions, photos if available, and a bit about the inspiration behind your dish.
Where can I buy your hot sauces?
Our sauces are available in select specialty stores across the West Coast and through our online store. Check our "Products" page for a full list of retailers and our online shop.
Do you offer cooking classes or events?
Yes! We host monthly virtual cooking classes and occasional in-person events in Portland. Check our "Events" page for the current schedule and ticket information.
